# Privacy Policy for ob Libyan Speed

**Last Updated:** January 24, 2026

## 1. Introduction
ob Libyan Speed ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application and services.

## 2. Information We Collect
- **Location Data:** We collect precise location data to facilitate ride matching and tracking, even when the app is in the background (for drivers).
- **Personal Info:** Name, Phone Number, Email (optional).
- **Financial Info:** Transaction history and wallet balance.
- **Device Info:** Device ID, IP address, and usage logs.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- To provide ride-hailing services.
- To process payments and refunds.
- To enhance safety via our Guardian System (route deviation monitoring).
- To comply with local legal obligations (KYC/AML).

## 4. Data Sharing
We do not sell your data. We share data only with:
- **Drivers:** Your pickup/dropoff location and first name.
- **Authorities:** When required by law or for emergency response.

## 5. Security
We use SSL pinning, encryption at rest, and atomic transaction logs to protect your data.

## 6. Contact Us
For privacy concerns, contact: privacy@oblns.ly
